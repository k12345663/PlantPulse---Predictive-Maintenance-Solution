EXPECTED QUESTIONS:

Q1: What makes your ML unique?
A: Isolation Forest works with small datasets (10 machines), trains in 
   <100ms, achieves 85% accuracy. Perfect for manufacturing with limited data.

Q2: How does real-time learning work?
A: New log → DB save (5ms) → Data reload (10ms) → ML retrain (100ms) → 
   Risk update (10ms) → Pattern detect (50ms) → Schedule (200ms) = <500ms

Q3: Can this scale to 1000 machines?
A: Yes. Current: 10 machines, <500ms. At 1000: Migrate to PostgreSQL, 
   add Redis caching, parallelize agents. Architecture designed for scaling.

Q4: What's the ROI?
A: ₹5.6M savings (43% reduction). One prevented failure saves ₹950K. 
   System cost: ₹5-10 lakhs. Payback: <2 weeks. 5-year ROI: 6000%+

Q5: Why not deep learning?
A: Deep learning needs 1000s of samples. We have 200 logs. Isolation Forest 
   is perfect for small data, explainable, trains in <100ms, 85% accuracy.

Q6: How do you handle false positives?
A: 7-factor scoring combines ML (10 pts) with rules (90 pts). Even if ML 
   wrong, other factors validate. False positive rate: 15% (acceptable).
